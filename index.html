Welcome to my website! I teach in the Freshman Chemistry Program at Georgia Tech (officially, I'm the lab coordinator). My interests are in organic chemistry, chemical education, and educational technology. On this site you can find my teaching portfolio and educational technology projects. Enjoy!

Why "Metallacycle"?

Metallacycles are molecules that contain a metal atom embedded in a ring of atoms. As I've always had sort of a pet interest in organometallic chemistry, I decided to pick up the "metallacycle" domain name. I'm also looking forward to Strem dropping a huge wad of cash on me to buy it up! (Kidding...)
