CHEM 332, Elementary Organic Chemistry II summer 2009 - spring 2013

I have created a wide variety of materials for CHEM 332, which is a heavily student-centered, technology-enhanced organic chemistry II course. Most of these materials are freely available on the web. For a project that involved student creation of wiki pages (described in detail here), I developed a series of technical Frequently Asked Questions, a web interface for Jmol and MarvinSketch, a grading rubric for the project, and other administrative materials for the project's wiki space. CHEM 492, Organic Chemistry Refresher Course for High School Teachers fall 2009 - fall 2011

CHEM 492 was an organic chemistry refresher course for high school teachers across the state of Illinois, taught as a component of the ICLCS. The course was run completely online, with student-instructor interactions occurring through a Moodle forum. I prepared online materials for the course and monitored the Moodle forums daily.

CHEM 437, Advanced Organic Chemistry Laboratory spring 2009

CHEM 437 is an advanced laboratory course. I served as a teaching assistant for this course during my first year in graduate school. For one of the midterms, I prepared some problems at the instructor's request. You can download the problems in Word document format here.

CHEM 236, Fundamental Organic Chemistry I fall 2008

For this course, I prepared a number of independent study materials for students. These mostly condense information about reactions covered in class into short, readable vignettes. You can download the full set of materials I prepared for CHEM 236 here. The full set of notes prepared by the instructor is also available here.
